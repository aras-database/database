Notes

This list is a tool for observers of Symbiotic Stars (photometry, spectroscopy)

The list of stars is extracted from the New Online Symbiotic Database Merc, J., Gális, R., Wolf, M. (2019) considered as "galactic confirmed".

V, B: magnitudes V and B from SIMBAD
GP,BP: magnitudes from GAIA
Mag is the last measure obtained in AAVSO database in Band V, Visible, CV, TG
Dlast is the number of days since the last observation in bands V, Visible, CV, TG
